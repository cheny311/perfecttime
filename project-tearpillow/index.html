1 — Concept

Contact and release: The pillow is designed as a vessel for emotions that are often kept silent.

When held or squeezed, it triggers gentle sounds and close-up footage of fabric absorbing water. The softness contrasts with the heaviness of the feeling.

Use this space to explain the themes of care, comfort, and ritual that surround the object.
