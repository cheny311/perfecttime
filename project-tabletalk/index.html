0 — Overview

Table Talk is an interactive installation about how authority speaks, and how silence is produced in everyday spaces.
It takes place around a kitchen table.

The system speaks.
It pauses.
It resumes.

Most of the time, it stands there unnoticed. You don't realize you're part of it— until you do. By then, leaving is no longer the point.

1 — What happens at the table

English

The table is staged like a familiar kitchen scene.

A voice keeps speaking.

A pan sits on the stove. A basket of ingredients nearby. Printed "recipes" are laid out.

The recipes give instructions—like everyday cooking guides.

Except the ingredients and the way you're asked to handle them aren't quite everyday.

When ingredients go into the pan, voices arrive.

Different actions trigger different responses.

Some voices are directional—only for the person standing right in front of them.

Others stay in the room no matter where you stand.

At certain moments, the recipe tells you to interact back.

Raising your voice can interrupt what's happening—briefly.

It doesn't ask. It doesn't explain. It keeps running.

You can follow, hesitate, interrupt, or stop mid-action.

None of those choices truly shut the system down.

2 — How the system works

中文

《Table Talk》通过一组简单、基于规则的互动机制运作。

桌边的物理行为会触发声音与影像的变化。系统会监听、测量，并作出回应。

重量触发：重量传感器检测食材是否被放入锅中。不同重量区间对应不同的声音与影像状态。

有的评论关于食材是否新鲜、有多"合适"；有的直接指向你作为一个人的行为规范。

音量触发：桌旁有一个立着的"耳朵"，里面放着麦克风监测环境音量。

当音量超过阈值，系统会暂停当前内容。但这种暂停是暂时的。

短暂间隔后，系统会重新开始——并告诉你"你反应好大"。

身体中断：角落里有一个很大的软雕塑人脸。

参与者把手伸进鼻孔，可以拿到两张小脸（小笑脸里嵌入压力传感器）。

当它被按下时，所有声音与影像会同时停止。房间进入黑屏/静默。

但系统并不会关闭。延迟之后，它回到默认状态。

当下一个人靠近桌子，声音又开始——甚至会对"上一个人"做评价：你不要像她那样。

系统不记住人，也不会学习。它只遵循规则，并继续运作。

English

Table Talk runs on a simple, rule-based set of interactions.

Physical actions at the table trigger changes in sound and image. The system listens, measures, and responds.

Weight trigger: A load cell detects when ingredients are placed into the pan.

Different weight ranges map to different voice + video states.

Some comments are about the ingredient—freshness, "proper" handling. Others are about you.

Volume trigger: A standing "ear" contains a microphone that monitors ambient loudness.

When the volume crosses a threshold, the system pauses the current content—temporarily.

After a short delay, it resumes and tells you you "overreacted."

Body interruption: In the corner, there is a large soft face sculpture.

When participants reach into its nostrils, they can hold two small faces embedded with pressure sensors.

Pressing them stops all sound and image at once. The room drops into blackout / silence.

But the system doesn't shut down. After a delay, it returns to default.

When the next person approaches the table, the voice begins again—sometimes commenting on the person before: don't be like her.

The system doesn't remember people. It doesn't learn. It follows rules and keeps running.

3 — Moving through the space

The room is divided into three zones.

They are not arranged to guide you gently through the space. They face each other.

Projection Zone

A close-up video of a man's mouth is projected onto the wall. It speaks continuously.

Only the person standing directly in front hears the scolding clearly. Others hear a mumble.

Technically: MetaHuman (UE5) lip sync + AI voice, with a green-painted face as a symbol of replaceability.

Cooking Zone

Directly facing the projection is a kitchen table.

Ingredients placed into the pan trigger different voices and visuals. A microphone can pause the system—briefly.

After the pause, it resumes and comments on your reaction.

Face Interaction Zone

In a corner, slightly away from the main axis, there is a large soft sculpture of a human face.

Inside the nostrils are two smaller faces embedded with pressure sensors. Pressing them stops everything at once.

After a delay, the system returns. When the next person approaches the table, the voice begins again.
